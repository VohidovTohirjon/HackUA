# BearPath

BearPath is a graduation GPS that helps students catch hidden degree-rule risks before they become graduation delays.

Built for the University of Arizona Bear Down, Build Up undergraduate education hackathon track.

## Short Pitch

Students may think they are on track to graduate, but hidden rules like Gen Eds, electives, prerequisites, upper-division credits, transfer-credit mismatches, course availability, and advising timing can quietly delay them. BearPath turns those hidden risks into visible next steps.

## Problem

Many students understand their major requirements, but not the full degree system around them. This can be especially confusing for international, transfer, and first-generation students who are also learning new terminology, advising expectations, and institutional processes.

## Solution

BearPath shows a student-friendly Graduation GPS, explains hidden degree rules in plain language, creates a rescue route, sends the risk summary to an advisor, and helps leadership see repeated system blockers.

## Main Features

- Sofia's student story and Graduation GPS route
- Hidden Rules Explainer for Gen Ed, electives, prerequisites, upper-division credits, transfer credits, bottlenecks, and course availability
- Graduation Risk Rescue Plan with next best actions
- Advisor alert and intervention queue
- Admin System Blockers Dashboard
- Demo Story page for the final hackathon pitch

## 3-Minute Demo Flow

1. Open Student View and show Sofia's story.
2. Click Check My Graduation Risk.
3. Show Graduation GPS and Rescue Plan.
4. Click Send to Advisor.
5. Open Advisor View and show the alert/intervention queue.
6. Open Admin View and show system blockers.
7. Open Demo Story and explain iteration.
